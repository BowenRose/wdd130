History

When I heard the story of my family's journey to the Americas via a raft, I knew my life's purpose. I decided to start a rafting business to honor my ancestors' trials and tribulations, giving my family and others a better chance at a good life. Now, I use this opportunity to provide others with the chance to have fun with rafting.
