History

When I heard the story of my families journey to the Americas via a raft I knew my life's purpose. I decided to start a rafting business to pay amage to my ancestors trials and tribulation in order to give me and my family the best chance at a better life. Now I use this opportunity to provide others with the chance to use rafting to have fun.
