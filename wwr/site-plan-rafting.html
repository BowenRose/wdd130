Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide a palatable service to rafters. This will be done by making the website easily navigated.

Audience

The target audience includes people rafting in our area, typically younger individuals who enjoy the outdoors. Here are three scenarios to consider:

  • Scenario 1: A group of friends looking for exciting rafting trips during the summer.
  • Scenario 2: A family planning a weekend adventure that includes rafting and other outdoor activities.
  • Scenario 3: A solo rafter seeking information on safety and gear for a solo rafting experience.
