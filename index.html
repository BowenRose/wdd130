Idaho

Idaho, known as the "Gem State," is located in the northwestern region of the United States. It is renowned for its stunning natural landscapes, which include mountains, forests, and rivers. The state's diverse geography is characterized by the Rocky Mountains in the east, the Snake River Plain in the south, and the beautiful lakes and rivers that traverse the land. The state capital, Boise, is the largest city in Idaho and serves as a cultural and economic hub.
